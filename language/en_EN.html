Manual Lightspeed-plugin


Introduction

Create your shipping labels automatically using the Lightspeed plug-in. Using this link, you can create four types of MyParcel shipments:

  • Parcel shipments (including ‘Collect from PostNL location’)
  • Mailbox parcel
  • Unfranked label
The prices displayed are just examples. Actual rates may vary. For up-to-date prices, please consult the rates in your MyParcel account.

1. Installation

1.1 New installation

You can find the MyParcel plug-in in the backoffice of your Lightspeed webshop. Click Apps in the menu on the left-hand side and then search for MyParcel in the top right-hand corner. When you click the MyParcel Nederland plug-in (recognisable by the small PostNL logo in the upper right corner), you will be directed to the information page for that plug-in. Click Install app in the top right-hand corner to create the link.

Before you can proceed to configuration, the MyParcel plug-in needs permission to read and process information. To grant permission, you need to log in on the screen below with the login details for your Lightspeed environment.

1.2 Updating an existing installation

As a MyParcel app user, you don’t need to worry about updating the app. The app is updated automatically, which means you’re always using the most recent version.

1.3 Frequently asked questions about the installation

Why does the MyParcel plug-in need to read and process my order?

In order to create an export to MyParcel, the plug-in needs permission to read the information. Once the barcode is pre-registered, we will link it back to the Lightspeed order. Since this involves a change to the order, these permissions are required.

2. Configuration

2.1 Standard export settings, shipping and delivery options

In order to configure the MyParcel app, go to the home screen in the backoffice of your Lightspeed webshop and click Apps. Click App Store on the left-hand side. Then search for MyParcel in the search bar in the top right-hand corner. Click the MyParcel app to open the app’s information page and then click Go to app. The configuration page will open in a new tab.

API-key
To export shipments to your MyParcel account, you need an API key. You can find it on your MyParcel backoffice by clicking the symbol in the top right-hand corner. Then go to Settings > Webshops > General.

When to forward to MyParcel?
Exports can be performed automatically by reading details from orders. You can export orders depending on both the shipping method (see chapter 2.2) and the shipping status in Lightspeed. Select Orders with ‘Not sent’ status if you want to export orders to MyParcel automatically. They will appear in your MyParcel backoffice automatically within a few minutes. You do not need to do anything once they appear in the backoffice. If you would prefer to export orders to MyParcel manually, you can select Orders with ‘Sent’ status. You need to set the shipment status to Sent before exporting the order to MyParcel.

Shipping options
If you want to offer a specific shipping option as standard for all parcel shipments with destinations in the Netherlands (e.g. insured shipment or signature upon receipt), you can select it here. Please note that there are additional costs for some shipping options. You can calculate the shipping costs in your MyParcel backoffice at the top of the page, under Finance > Calculate rates.

You can also choose to check that the recipient is aged 18 or over on delivery by switching on the Age Check option.

Delivery options
You can also let your customers select additional delivery options. Here you can set the delivery options that your customers can select. NB: since the delivery options are shipping methods in themselves, enter the total shipping costs here.

If you would prefer not to show a date for the morning and evening delivery options, select the 'Do not show delivery date at checkout' option.

Here you can indicate the days you offer shipments to PostNL and the minimum order amount to qualify for free delivery.

2.2 Parcel preferences

In order to display the correct delivery options at the checkout, you need to configure shipping methods. To create a shipping method, click Settings in the left-hand menu, and then go to Shop settings and Shipping methods. You will now see a summary of your shipping methods. To create a new shipping method, click Add shipping method manually. To export a parcel shipment, select ‘Standard shipment’ in the next screen and click ‘Continue’. The title that now appears is very important. The title needs to contain a ‘trigger’ word. The link can identify several words and thereby understand that the following need to be created:

PostNL: Parcel
Brievenbus: Mailbox parcel
Ongefrankeerd: Unfranked label
Bol.com: Parcel
Exact: Parcel
Mailbox: Mailbox parcel
Frankering: Unfranked label
Postzegel: Digital stamp
Please note when using the digital stamp: The postage is determined on the basis of the total weight of the products in the order. If no weights are entered for the products, the shipment will receive postage for 0-20 grams. You can still adjust this per shipment in the back office.

 Examples of shipping method titles you can use: Verzending via PostNL Brievenbuspakje Shipment via PostNL.


After entering the title, you can add a description that will be showed under the shipping method at the checkout. Then click Add shipping rates to add a shipping rate. Enter a rate and the applicable weights for that rate. MyParcel charges a standard rate for parcels weighing 0-23kg and an XL rate for parcels weighing 23-30kg. Once you have set the rates, click Save. Select the countries for which the shipping method applies underCountries and select how the shipping method will be taxed under ‘Tax rate’. Once you have finished configuring the settings, click Save in the top right-hand corner.

2.3 Collect from a PostNL location

To display Collect from a PostNL location at your checkout, the standard PostNL plug-in is required. First of all, click Settings in the left-hand menu and then Shipping methods. Then click PostNL settings and switch on integration in the Login information section. The details below do not need to be entered!

Scroll down and switch on the Collect from a PostNL location option under Shipping methods. Scroll down and enter your details under Sender address. Save these settings before proceeding to the last section in the settings.

Lastly, you need to add a rate for Collect from a PostNL location . If you do not do this, the delivery option will not be visible at the checkout. Click Add prices at the bottom of the page to add a rate. Then select the ‘PostNL - PakjeGemak’ shipping option and Allow shipping method to activate the shipping method. Enter the shipping costs and Countries and save the page. Your customers can now select a PostNL location at the checkout!

2.4 Frequently asked questions about configuring the plug-in

How do I change the delivery options at my checkout?

In order to turn delivery options on and off or to change the rate, go to the home screen in the backoffice of your Lightspeed webshop and click Apps. Click App Store on the left-hand side. Then search for MyParcel in the search bar in the top right-hand corner. Click the MyParcel app to open the app’s information page and then click Go to app. \ The configuration page will open in a new tab.

When do customers receive a discount for delivery options?

Within Lightspeed, you can offer free shipping for standard shipping methods. To give you more flexibility, we work with a separate discount for each delivery option. You can offer your customer free shipping for orders over €50. They still pay for the extra service they want to purchase. In the example below, evening delivery is activated. The customer pays €7.30 for this shipping method as standard, but the rate is reduced to €1.25 for orders over €50.

3. Use of the plugin

3.1 Creating one shipping label

To select which orders to export to MyParcel, set the option ‘When to forward to MyParcel?’ from chapter 2.1 to Orders with ‘Sent’ status . This allows you to export orders individually and decide when to export them.

3.2 Creating multiple shipping labels

To create multiple orders at the same time, set the option ‘When to forward to MyParcel?’ to Orders with ‘Not sent’ status . All new orders will be exported directly to MyParcel.

3.3 Making changes to one shipment

The shipping or delivery options in a Lightspeed order that has already been created cannot be changed. However, you can change these options in the MyParcel backoffice by clicking the symbol next to the shipment.

3.4 Frequently asked questions the use of the plug-in

Why has my shipment not been exported to MyParcel?

If you can’t find a shipment in the MyParcel back office, it is important to check that the shipment has the correct shipping status (sent/not sent, depending on your settings in chapter 2.1) in the Lightspeed order or that there is a 'trigger' word in the shipment method. Otherwise, the order will not be exported to MyParcel.

Alternatively, the details entered by your customer may not be recognisable by our system. A letter might be missing from a Dutch postcode or there may be more than six characters in the suffix field, for example.
